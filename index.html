    Welcome to CustomsByNico

    Hi! My name is Nicolette, but you can call me Nico! CustomsByNico is a global business that strives to create quality, hand-painted shoes, allowing anyone to express themselves through their fashion. Check out my socials to follow my journey and stay up to date with upcoming drops!

    March 2020

    Founded in Mt. Vernon, NY, CustomsByNico started as a hobby picked up at the beginning of the Covid-19 pandemic. After seeing people paint their clothing and shoes on TikTok, I decided to try this out for myself. After some trial and error, I painted my first pair of Air Force Ones. Friends and family took notice of this new hobby and began asking for customs for themselves.

    September 2020

    After months of practicing my painting skills and learning how to post engaging content on Instagram and TikTok, I finally got my first order!

    September 2021

    Reached 100 Orders!

    January 2022

    10,000 followers on TikTok!

    November 2025

    With the drive to elevate CustomsByNico to the next level, CustomsByNico will begin to expand its product portfolio. A clothing line and small accessories to come!
